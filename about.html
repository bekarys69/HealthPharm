How We Started

The initial stage of creating a network of pharmacies "HealthPharm" was associated with one of the largest kazakh distributors of pharmacological drugs "Zerde". The first pharmacies opened by this company in 2011 became an experimental direction for developing advanced marketing technologies. After the successful development of the marketing policy, the company's management decided to separate pharmacies into an independent network.

We Are Trusted Company

"HealthPharm" is not only a chain of pharmacies, it is a team of professionals, advanced technologies of the pharmacy business, a wide range and only high-quality products. Here you can find the necessary medications - from aspirin to rare medications, buy baby food or home medical devices, and choose medical cosmetics and hygiene products with the help of experienced consultants. The pharmacy "HealthPharm" always there for you!

Free Shipping
